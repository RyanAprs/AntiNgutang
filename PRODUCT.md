# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

Friend groups and coworkers eating together — anyone who needs to split a restaurant bill fairly. The primary user is at the table right after a meal, phone in hand, wanting a quick resolution so nobody has to be the "bendahara dadakan" (impromptu treasurer). No account, no setup, no typing items by hand.

## Product Purpose

Eliminate the friction of splitting bills after group meals. Scan a receipt photo, tap who ordered what, and get a fair split — proportional tax and service charge included — ready to copy and share. Success looks like: the group's bill is settled in under a minute without anyone pulling out a calculator.

## Positioning

Zero typing, fair proportional splits, and no account required — the fastest path from receipt to settlement.

## Brand Personality

Playful · Helpful · Clean. Friendly and approachable without being cartoonish. The tone is casual Indonesian warmth — like a helpful friend who handles the math so nobody has to be the bendahara dadakan. Confident but not corporate, playful but not gamified.

## Anti-references

- Overly gamified or cartoonish bill splitters (too playful)
- Corporate banking or formal finance apps (too serious)
- Generic SaaS dashboard aesthetics

## Design Principles

1. **Zero friction** — no typing, no accounts, no setup. Every tap removes a step, never adds one.
2. **Fair by default** — proportional splits are automatic and transparent. Every number is explainable.
3. **Mobile-first, thumb-friendly** — designed for one hand on a phone at a restaurant table. Big targets, bottom-sheet modals, minimal scroll.
4. **Playful confidence** — friendly and warm without being childish. The brand smiles but doesn't joke.
5. **Trust through clarity** — every person sees exactly what they owe and why. No hidden math.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA compliance. Adequate contrast ratios, focus indicators, reduced motion support, and readable font sizes throughout.
